Teen · Clear aligners · 18 months

The art of precision.

Child · Phase I · Palatal expander · 8 months

Early intervention, lifelong impact.

A young patient with a posterior crossbite and functional shift. Phase I treatment with a palatal expander guided proper jaw development and restored a healthy bite — the ADA recommends a first orthodontic evaluation by age 7.

Child–Teen · Phase I + II · Facemask & braces · 30 months

The power of growth modification.

Two-phase, growth-guided care — a facemask to direct jaw development, followed by braces to finish. No teeth pulled, just perfect timing and guided growth.

Teen · Braces · 20 months

The gap is gone, the bite is right.

We closed the diastema and corrected the overbite for that seamless, lasting alignment — small details, big transformation.

Teen · Braces · 20 months

Spaces closed, bite refined.

Correcting the overbite and closing the spaces for a balanced, even smile — refined down to the very last detail.

Teen · Clear aligners · 12 months

A wider smile changes everything.

Gently expanding and aligning the arches reduced the dark buccal corridors and created a fuller, broader, more balanced smile — orthodontics isn't just straight teeth, it's designing the smile frame.

Adult · Braces · 24 months

Restoring function and esthetics.

An upper-premolar extraction case to reduce excessive overjet — rounding out a triangular archform, achieving an ideal Class I bite, and leveling a deep bite for lasting function and beauty.
